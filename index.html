Simple 21 Day Tracker

A simple progress tracker for 21 Day Fix

View project on GitHub

Keep track of your success

My Simple 21 Day Log lets you easily keep track your progress through the 21 Day Fix diet program. This simple application requires minimal setup to begin meeting your daily goals. Head to the calendar to see your weekly success through the program. Download now!

Features

  • Keep a daily log of your progress by clicking on the appropriate cup color as referenced in the 21 Day Fix program.
  • The calendar will provide a simple and usable interface for you to track your monthly progress through the 21 Day Fix program.
  • The calendar days will indicate whether the user met the program goals for that day.
  • The settings section will automatically calculate the intended calorie target for you and suggest recommend goals that relate to the 21 Day Fix program.
